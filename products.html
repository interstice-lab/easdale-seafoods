Live Langoustine (Nephrops norvegicus)

Langoustine Gallery

Overview

Scottish live langoustines—also called Dublin Bay prawns—are prized for their delicate sweet flavor and tender, succulent texture. Harvested in creel pots off Scotland’s west coast, each is handled with care from sea to table.

Key Characteristics

Fishing Method
Creel pots—an eco-friendly approach minimizing habitat impact.
Handling & Transport
Stored in individual cells to protect from light, noise, and temperature.
Sizes
XL, L, M, S—warmer months yield smaller catches.
Availability
Year-round, peaking Feb–Apr and quiet Sept–Nov.
Culinary Uses
Grill, boil, or add to pasta, risotto and salads.
Traceability
Each box is linked to a specific boat for full provenance.

Live Razor Clams (Ensis siliqua)

Razor Clam Gallery

Overview

Our razor clams—locally called “spoots”—are prized for their sweet, delicate flavor and firm yet tender texture. Hand-collected by divers and graded ashore, each clam meets the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

We export extensively to Singapore and Hong Kong, where our eco-friendly methods are highly valued.

Key Characteristics

Species
Ensis siliqua, E. magnus, E. ensis
Habitat
Sandy & muddy sediments along UK coasts
Harvest Methods
Hand-dive, licensed dredges, and controlled electrofishing.
Sustainability
Hand-harvested to minimize environmental impact
Nutritional Highlights
Rich in lean protein, B-vitamins, calcium, iron & phosphorus
Culinary Uses
Steam, pan-fry, roast, or add to chowders and pasta

Scottish Lobster

Overview

Harvested in Scotland’s pristine coastal waters, our lobsters boast sweet, succulent meat and a firm yet tender bite, with subtle oceanic notes.

Caught in creel pots and graded by hand to guarantee peak quality.

Key Characteristics

Habitat
Cold, clean waters on hard ground in shallow Scottish seas
Sustainability
Protected by minimum carapace length regulations
Seasonality
Best enjoyed in June–July

How to Enjoy

  • Grilled/Steamed: Highlights its natural sweetness
  • Lobster Rolls: Perfect for on-the-go indulgence
  • Summer Salads: Combine with scallops & greens
  • Hand-Cut Chips: A hearty classic pairing
  • Starter or Main: Equally elegant in any course
