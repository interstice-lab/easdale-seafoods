Our Clients

Wholesale & Export

We ship live langoustines, lobsters and razor clams across Europe and Asia — with full traceability, export documentation, and custom packing formats.

Our clients include seafood wholesalers and premium distributors in France (Vendée, Brittany, Paris, Toulouse), Spain, and high-end seafood importers in Hong Kong and Singapore.

We are actively expanding our small-scale customer base in France and Spain — and welcome enquiries from fishmongers, restaurants, and independent buyers looking for direct-from-source Scottish shellfish.

Restaurants & Chefs

We are proud to serve a number of chefs and restaurants — from local kitchens in Argyll to Michelin-level cuisine in London and beyond. We understand the expectations of professional kitchens, and our catch is handled accordingly: fresh, sustainable, and consistent.

Whether you’re a seasonal restaurant by the coast or a fine-dining venue in the city, we can advise on species, size grading, and availability throughout the year.

Local Customers – Scotland
