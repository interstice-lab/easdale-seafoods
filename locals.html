Our Local Partners

At Easdale Seafoods, we believe the strongest partnerships spring from shared landscapes, seasons, and values.

For over 30 years, we’ve collaborated with local restaurants who honour quality, simplicity, and integrity. Our shellfish is caught daily by small creel boats in the clear waters surrounding Easdale and delivered straight to their kitchens.

  • Tigh an Truish Inn

    An iconic 2AA Rosette restaurant beside the historic Bridge Over the Atlantic. Renowned for refined, seasonal cuisine and featured on MasterChef: The Professionals.

    Visit Website →

  • Oyster Bar, Ellenabeich

    A warm, family-run spot offering fresh shellfish and locally farmed produce in one of Scotland’s most scenic west-coast villages.
