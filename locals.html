Easdale Seafoods Ltd

Local Customers

At Easdale Seafoods, we’ve always believed that the strongest partnerships are rooted in place — in shared landscapes, seasons, and values.

For over 30 years, we’ve worked hand-in-hand with local restaurants who, like us, honour quality, simplicity, and integrity. Our shellfish is caught daily by small creel boats from the clear waters around Easdale, and delivered directly to kitchens just minutes from our harbour.

We’re proud to supply two of the finest establishments on the Isle of Seil:

  • Tigh an Truish Inn — An iconic 2AA Rosette restaurant beside the historic Bridge Over the Atlantic, known for its refined, seasonal cuisine, and featured on Masterchef: The Professionals.
  • Oyster Bar, Ellenabeich — A welcoming family-run restaurant offering fresh shellfish and locally farmed produce in one of the most scenic spots on the west coast.

These partnerships are built on trust, quality, and a shared respect for what the land and sea provide.
