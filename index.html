About Us

Easdale Seafoods is a small, family-run business based on the West Coast of Scotland. Since 1990, we have specialised in the export of live shellfish — with a focus on quality, sustainability, and trust.

We source exclusively from local 'creel' boats operating in the clear waters surrounding Easdale, ensuring gentle handling and top-grade freshness at every stage of the process.

Our products are delivered to wholesalers, chefs, and distributors across Europe and beyond — with the care and consistency only a close-knit team can provide.
